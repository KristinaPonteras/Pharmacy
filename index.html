Welcome to Lady of All Nations Pharmacy

Lady of All Nations Pharmacy & General Merchandise provides essential healthcare products and over-the-counter medicines to the community of Cordova, Cebu. We ensure safe, reliable, and affordable access to medications while offering general merchandise for everyday needs.

We combine compassion and operational improvements to focus on patient safety and community outreach. Our proposed technology improvements aim to free staff time and reduce errors while improving reporting and stock control.

System Proposal

Problems & Limitations
  • Manual reconciliation of daily sales is time-consuming and error-prone.
  • Inventory management for OTC and supplies relies on visual/manual checks.
  • Expiry date monitoring requires tedious manual work.
  • Special requests may be lost if not properly tracked.
  • PMS system is complex and not user-friendly for simple tasks.
Proposed Solutions
  • Automated POS Integration for accurate and real-time sales reporting.
  • Barcode-based Inventory Management with reorder suggestions.
  • Automated cycle counting & expiry monitoring with alerts.
  • Digital tracking for special requests and reminders.
  • User-friendly reporting dashboard for quick insights.
Benefits
  • Saves time through faster inventory & checkout.
  • Reduces errors in expiry & stock control.
  • Improves sales tracking with integrated data.
  • Enables staff to focus on patient care.
Conclusion

Implementing automation in Lady of All Nations Pharmacy will enhance operational efficiency, reduce human errors, and allow staff to dedicate more time to personalised patient care and community outreach.
